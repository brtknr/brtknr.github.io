Hi, Bharat here! Welcome to my [website][website]. I live in Bristol (United Kingdom) and I am about to finish my doctoral training at University of Bristol on [mass evacuation modelling][massevac] using open geospatial data sources like [OpenStreetMap][osm]. I am also currently immersed in applying [deep reinforcement learning][drl] to optimise policy of interaction between connected autonomous vehicles at [Airbus Group Innovations][agi] under the umbrella of [Flourish][flourish] consortium. Prior to Bristol, I was living in Bath until 2012 to complete an undergraduate Masters degree in Civil and Architectural Engineering at University of Bath. 

I like spending my time sitting in coffee shops, learning new things, organising, travelling and collaborating on coding projects (hint hint)!
